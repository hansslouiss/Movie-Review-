Matrix

Released in 1999

This movie is about a man coming to a realization that his world isn't real. He decide to join a team that showed him the world and how to escape.

Fun fact about the movie

  1. The opening action scene took six months of training and four days to shoot.
  2. In Greek Mythology Morpheus is the god of dreams. Somewhat ironic considering Morpheus’ role here is to awaken people from their dream states to reality.
  3. Neo is often referred to as the “One”, which is an anagram of “Neo”.
